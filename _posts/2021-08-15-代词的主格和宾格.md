**代词的主格和宾格**

# 主格代词

* I 
* he 
* she 
* it 
* you 
* we 
* they

1. I am a teacher.
2. He is a teacher.
3. You are teachers.


# 宾格代词

* me
* him
* her
* it
* you
* us
* them

1. He likes me.
2. We like her.
3. I like them.
