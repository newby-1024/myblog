**形容词性和名词性物主代词**

# 形容词性物主代词

## 单数形式
* my
* your
* his/her/its
* one's

# 复数形式
* our
* your
* their

This is **my** book.
We love **our** motherland.
Those are **your** socks.

# 名词性物主代词

## 单数形式
* mine
* yours
* his/her/its
* one's

## 复数形式
* ours
* yours
* theirs

The book is **ours**.
The apple is **hers**.
