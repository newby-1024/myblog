**be 动词的形式和用法**

*8种be动词：*
* be 
* is 
* am 
* are 
* was 
* were 
* being
* bean

be 动词后面接名词、形容词、地点副词或短语作补足语。

1. The man **is** a teacher.
2. Mary's new dresses **are** colorful.
3. My mother **was** in the kitchen.

练习：

1. 他们是老师。  
They <u>are</u> tearchers.

2. 他以前是一名老师。  
He <u>was</u> a teacher before.

3. 他已经当了 3 年的老师。  
He has <u>been</u> a teacher for 3 years.
