**be 动词的否定**

在 *am, is, are, was, were* 后面加 *not* 。

缩略式 *am not, isn't, aren't, wasn't, weren't* 。

be 动词提问和回答：

- Is he a teacher?
- Yes, he is. / No, he isn't.  
<br />

- Are you a teacher?
- Yes, I am./ No, I am not.
<br />

- Were they teachers?
- Yes, they were./ No, they weren't.
