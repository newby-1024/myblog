**过去进行时**

过去进行时表示在过去某一时刻或某一段时间内进行或发生的动作。

其形式为 was/were + doing。

1. They **were waiting** for you.

2. He **was talking** with his friends just now.

练习：

Sam <u>was watching</u> TV at 7:00 last night.
